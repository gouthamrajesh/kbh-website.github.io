About Us

Kyaa Baat Haai is the official college radio of Government Engineering College Barton Hill, Trivandrum. We started at aiming to produce a virtual platform to showcase the talents of our mates and growing as the official radio that spreaded across various social media platforms.

  • Enable everyone to showcase the skills virtually.
  • Broadcasting the whole college with daily updates.
  • Regularly holds talent enhancing programs and celebrity talks along with Podcast Radio sessions becoming an inevitable part of GECB-ians lives.

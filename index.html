About Us

Kyaa Baat Haai is a Student's Radio of Government Engineering College Barton Hill, Trivandrum. We began with small steps, aiming to establish a virtual platform that could showcase the talents and creativity of our mates. With an enthusiastic team and many active supporters, KBH is today a favourite virtual radio, available across various social media platforms.
